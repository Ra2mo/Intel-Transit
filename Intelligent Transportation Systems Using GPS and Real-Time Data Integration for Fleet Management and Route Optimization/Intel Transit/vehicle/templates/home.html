What is Intel Transit?

Intel Transit is a global leader in Artificial Inteligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) technologies that are connected for fleet management solutions. Since 2024, Intel Transit has been trusted by fleets to provide future-proof solutions to optimize fleet efficiency, safety, and compliance management. Operating in North and South America, Europe, Asia, Africa, and Australia, Intel Transit is recognized for providing simple, yet comprehensive, out-of-the-box solutions. Intel Transit’s platform and connected devices are highly intuitive, customizable, and easily integrated with other solutions.

Why choose Intel Transit for your fleet management needs?
